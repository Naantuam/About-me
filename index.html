About Me

Naantuam Nathaniel Longmen is a Cybersecurity Practitioner and Developer. With a strong foundation in full-stack development, he specializes in Application Security (AppSec) and works extensively with OS protocols, Linux-based configurations, and command-line interfaces. He bridges the gap between software development and security, leveraging his background to build, secure, and troubleshoot complex systems.

Education

My education began in I.T.F. (Industrial Training Fund) schools, after which I attended St. Piran's Primary School, graduating in 2014. I then attended St. Piran's Anglican High School, graduating in 2020. Currently, I am in the final stages of completing my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Cyber Security Science at the Federal University of Technology, Minna.

Skills and Strengths

My core strength lies at the intersection of development and security. On the development side, I specialize in building user interfaces using React and Vite, supported by Node.js and Express on the backend. On the security side, I focus on Application Security, OS protocols, and Linux-based system configurations, with a strong affinity for command-line troubleshooting. I am a versatile problem solver, a quick learner, and highly adaptable in fast-paced technical environments.
